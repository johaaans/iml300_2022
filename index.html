projects

uno

I love music and I had fun experimenting with the webcam attributes. I wanted to make a fun, interactive playlist, while also explore the possibilities of mapping and pixel control.

deux

I wanted to create a hypernarrative that could only be told with time and space attributes. Along the way of conceiving the project to life I realized it would be more difficult than I thought and had to simplify it. While the project is still a work in progress, it is a something I would like to finish the way I intended and include the interactability I had planned. I learned a lot during the process and will definitely learn more to get it finished. The narrative of the project however, centers on the internal thoughts, memories and feelings of two characters from a story I created. I had hoped that this project could leave some sort of impact in the user by having complete control of the interaction.

three

In project three I wanted to explore the complexity of language and how it can unify a community of people while also be used as a tool for discrimination and colonialism. More specifically, I took excerpts from an essay I wrote on this topic in relation with the Spanish language, Latin America, and Spanish-speakers. I wanted to point out the nuances of language to inform and take a look into the future.
